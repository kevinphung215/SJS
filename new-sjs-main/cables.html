Structured Cabling- Be Well Connected

Over the years, SJS Access and Video has taken on more tenant improvement and new construction projects. As a result, we have been asked to provide our clients with all of their low voltage cabling needs including many structured cabling jobs in the Tri-State area. Let us handle all of your cabling needs and associated hardware to optimize your telephone, data and security networks to standardize all of your cabling systems with consistency so that the future cabling updates and troubleshooting will be easier to handle. We provide high-performance low voltage and data cable installation in the Tri-State area that will allow your network to operate to its full potential, with increased resilience and throughput. There are six areas of Structured Cabling, they are: Horizontal Cabling, Backbone Cabling, Work Areas, Telecommunications Closet (Room & Enclosure), Equipment Room Entrance to the Facility.
