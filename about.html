About Us

Since 2008, South Jersey Surveillance Systems has established a reputation as a reputable and reliable security electronics integrator, with emphasis on affordable pricing, quality products and installation, and competent customer service. From installing basic commercial and residential surveillance and alarm systems to fully integrated cloud-based enterprise class security solutions, we specialize in making life simpler, safer, and a far more rewarding.


Experience the Difference

Custom Solutions. Concierge Service.

Our client relationships are important to us — and we are committed to providing personal service that will exceed your expectations. Whether your project is large or small, we want to serve as your trusted advisor. We will listen to your goals, provide recommendations, and create a custom solution that works for your needs and budget.


Local, Reliable and Family-Owned.

It’s rewarding to select the best technology for your home or business — especially when it’s supported by a reliable and local team. As a private, family-owned South Jersey based business with a long history of serving the Greater Delaware Valley area, we provide dependable service that you can count on — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.


Advanced Technology. Simple Operation.

Electronics technology has come a long way — but even the most state-of-the-art systems are only useful if you know how to use them. At South Jersey Surveillance Systems, we select high quality systems with intuitive user interfaces, making it easy to use your security or automation system. With simple operation and detailed in-person training, we strive to ensure our customers are comfortable enjoying their new technology. And if you ever have a question, we’re just a phone call away.
